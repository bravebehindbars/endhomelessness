WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

OUR MISSION IS TO END HOMELESSNESS STATEWIDE

Homeless individuals are of every creed and color. No one is exempt from being inflicted with homelessness whether you are young or old. Our goal is help these indivuals who are struggling to find a service to help them. Finding shelters are the easiest part but what we want to do is lay down a better foundation for the indivuals so that he or she does not fall back in to being homeless.

WHAT ARE THE ROOT CAUSES?

1.Poverty, 2.Unemplyment, 3.Domestic Violence, 4.Mental Illness, 5.Addiction, 6.Alcholism, 7.Affordibale Housing, 8.Personal Hardships

HOW MANY CHILDREN ARE EFFECTED?

According to MainePublic.org, there are nearly 2,200 children facing housing insecurity this year.

HOW MANY ADULTS ARE EFFECTED?

According to 24/7wallst.com, there are currently 4,400 homeless adults in Maine.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We are an informational website that connects individuals to services that are there for the homeless to utilize in their communties.
